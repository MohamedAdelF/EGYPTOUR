## Common Questions & Answers

### Q: How accurate is the AI?
**A**: "Gemini 3 uses state-of-the-art vision and language models. It's trained on millions of images and can identify monuments with 95%+ accuracy. Plus, it's constantly learning."

### Q: Does it work offline?
**A**: "Core features work offline - your saved trips, missions, and photos. For AI features like Live Guide, you need internet connection, but we've optimized for low bandwidth."

### Q: What about privacy?
**A**: "All user data is stored securely in Firebase with proper authentication. Photos are stored in user's private cloud storage. We don't share data with third parties."

### Q: How does it compare to hiring a guide?
**A**: "It's free, available 24/7, speaks any language, personalized to your interests, and never gets tired. But we're not replacing guides - we're democratizing world-class guidance."

### Q: Can it scale to other countries?
**A**: "Absolutely! The architecture is designed for scaling. We just need to add location data and secrets for each country. Egypt is our proof of concept."
